A Digital Solution to Shift Perception and Empower Creative Talent in Africa

CINNARIOS

By harnessing technology, Cinnarios aims to break down barriers to art education and creative opportunities, making art a respected and attainable career path for all. We strive to spark a new wave of artistic expression that is relevant, inclusive, and forward-thinking.This mission is especially significant in Africa, where art is often undervalued as a career and as a vital part of life. By merging art with technology, Cinnarios seeks to challenge cultural stigmas, increase accessibility, and open new economic opportunities for young creatives. Our goal is to democratize art education, preserve cultural identity, and foster innovation, building a stronger and more inclusive creative industry across the continent.

About US

who are we?

Cinnarios is dedicated to transforming the perception of art from a luxury or mere entertainment into an essential, everyday part of life. Our mission is to make high-quality artistic education and skill development accessible to everyone, especially those in underserved communities, and to empower creatives to succeed regardless of their background. This model will lay the foundation for a larger project addressing the limited access to art education and upskilling resources in Africa.
