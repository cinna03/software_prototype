Get to know us a little bit more

Cinnarios is dedicated to transforming the perception of art from a luxury or mere entertainment into an essential, everyday part of life. Our mission is to make high-quality artistic education and skill development accessible to everyone, especially those in underserved communities, and to empower creatives to succeed regardless of their background. By harnessing technology, Cinnarios aims to break down barriers to art education and creative opportunities, making art a respected and attainable career path for all. We strive to spark a new wave of artistic expression that is relevant, inclusive, and forward-thinking. This mission is especially significant in Africa, where art is often undervalued as a career and as a vital part of life. By merging art with technology, Cinnarios seeks to challenge cultural stigmas, increase accessibility, and open new economic opportunities for young creatives. Our goal is to democratize art education, preserve cultural identity, and foster innovation, building a stronger and more inclusive creative industry across the continent.

How are we shifting the narrative?

The first phase, Cinnarios '001, will focus on showcasing art in advanced and meaningful ways (beyond entertainment or exclusivity) through powerful storytelling, articles, and documentary series. By highlighting real-life experiences of diverse artists, especially within the African community, we aim to dispel the myth that art is only for a select few. This model will lay the foundation for a larger project addressing the limited access to art education and upskilling resources in Africa.

4 values cinnarios is anchored in

1

Access Over Elitism

We believe creativity should be for everyone, not just the privileged few. We're breaking down barriers so that talent, not status, defines who gets to thrive.

2

Empowerment Through Education

Knowledge is power, and we're putting it in the hands of creatives. From skill-building to storytelling, we're creating pathways for artists to grow, lead, and own their craft.

3

Culture, Elevated

We don't just preserve culture — we amplify it. By honoring African identity and pushing creative boundaries, we're shaping a future where heritage and innovation go hand in hand.

4

Real over Perfect

No filters. No facades. We tell real stories, spotlight real people, and keep it authentic — because raw truth inspires real change.
